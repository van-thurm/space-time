Quick takes:
B (filled) and G (heavy outline) stay closest to the brutalist/sharp direction while solving the ambiguity.
D (ghost) is the quietest — good if you want buttons to recede.
E (underline) has a nice architectural quality that could pair well with the card grid.
C (pill) and H (circle) lean more iOS/friendly — might clash with the overall tone.
